# Banned Phrases вАФ Russian

These phrases often make drafts sound like generic AI output or dead marketing copy.

## Remove or rewrite

- `–Т–∞–ґ–љ–Њ –Њ—В–Љ–µ—В–Є—В—М, —З—В–Њ`
  - Delete and state the point directly.
- `–°–ї–µ–і—Г–µ—В –Њ—В–Љ–µ—В–Є—В—М`
  - Delete or replace with a concrete claim.
- `–°–ї–µ–і—Г–µ—В —Г—З–Є—В—Л–≤–∞—В—М`
  - Replace with an actual condition or fact.
- `–Т–∞–ґ–љ–Њ –њ–Њ–љ–Є–Љ–∞—В—М`
  - Delete and make the thought explicit.
- `–°—В–Њ–Є—В —Б–Ї–∞–Ј–∞—В—М`
  - Usually delete.
- `–Э–µ–Њ–±—Е–Њ–і–Є–Љ–Њ –њ–Њ–і—З–µ—А–Ї–љ—Г—В—М`
  - Usually delete.
- `–Э–µ –њ—А–Њ—Б—В–Њ X, –∞ Y`
  - Rebuild the sentence without the trick.
- `–Э–µ —В–Њ–ї—М–Ї–Њ X, –љ–Њ –Є Y`
  - Rewrite directly unless the contrast is genuinely needed.
- `–Т —Б–Њ–≤—А–µ–Љ–µ–љ–љ–Њ–Љ –Љ–Є—А–µ`
  - Start with a fact, scene, or argument instead.
- `–Т —Б–Њ–≤—А–µ–Љ–µ–љ–љ—Л—Е —А–µ–∞–ї–Є—П—Е`
  - Usually delete.
- `–Ъ–ї—О—З–µ–≤–∞—П —А–Њ–ї—М`
  - Name what matters and why.
- `–Ъ–ї—О—З–µ–≤–Њ–є –Љ–Њ–Љ–µ–љ—В`
  - State the moment itself.
- `–Т —Г—Б–ї–Њ–≤–Є—П—Е`
  - Often bureaucratic; rewrite more plainly.
- `–Т —Б–≤—П–Ј–Є —Б —Н—В–Є–Љ`
  - Use a more natural transition or none.
- `–Я—А–Њ–±–ї–µ–Љ–∞ –≤ –і—А—Г–≥–Њ–Љ`
  - Usually an artificial turn signal. Rewrite the next sentence directly.
- `–Т–Њ—В –≤ —З–µ–Љ –њ–Њ–і–≤–Њ—Е`
  - Usually a teaser phrase. State the point without announcing it.
- `–Ф–µ–ї–Њ –≤ —В–Њ–Љ`
  - Usually delete.
- `–Р —В–µ–Ї—Б—В –љ–∞—З–Є–љ–∞–µ—В—Б—П –њ–Њ–Ј–ґ–µ`
  - Meta-dramatic framing. Replace with the concrete action or claim.
- `–Ш –≤–Њ—В –њ–Њ—З–µ–Љ—Г`
  - Often unnecessary. Move straight to the reason.
- `–Я—А–Є —Н—В–Њ–Љ –≤–∞–ґ–љ–Њ –њ–Њ–љ–Є–Љ–∞—В—М`
  - Delete and say the thought plainly.
- `–Ч–і–µ—Б—М –≤–∞–ґ–љ–Њ –Њ—В–Љ–µ—В–Є—В—М`
  - Delete and state the point.
- `–Ь–Њ–ґ–µ—В –њ–Њ–≤–ї–Є—П—В—М`
  - Either say how it affects, or say uncertainty is real.
- `–Ь–Њ–ґ–µ—В —Б—В–∞—В—М`
  - Replace with a firmer claim if justified.
- `–Т –љ–µ–Ї–Њ—В–Њ—А—Л—Е —Б–ї—Г—З–∞—П—Е`
  - Keep only when the boundary really matters.
- `–Э–µ—А–µ–і–Ї–Њ`
  - Replace with a frequency you can defend.
- `–®–Є—А–Њ–Ї–Є–є –∞—Б—Б–Њ—А—В–Є–Љ–µ–љ—В`
  - Replace with a number, category, or real range.
- `–®–Є—А–Њ–Ї–Є–є —Б–њ–µ–Ї—В—А`
  - Replace with specifics.
- `–Я–µ—А—Б–Њ–љ–∞–ї–Є–Ј–Є—А–Њ–≤–∞–љ–љ—Л–є –њ–Њ–і—Е–Њ–і`
  - Explain what is personalized.
- `–Ш–љ–і–Є–≤–Є–і—Г–∞–ї—М–љ—Л–є –њ–Њ–і—Е–Њ–і`
  - Show the mechanism, not the label.
- `–Ш–љ–љ–Њ–≤–∞—Ж–Є–Њ–љ–љ—Л–µ —В–µ—Е–љ–Њ–ї–Њ–≥–Є–Є`
  - Name the technology.
- `–Ш–і–µ–∞–ї—М–љ–Њ–µ —А–µ—И–µ–љ–Є–µ`
  - Delete unless you can define вАЬidealвАЭ.
- `–£–љ–Є–Ї–∞–ї—М–љ—Л–є`
  - Usually empty; justify or cut.
- `–†—П–і –њ—А–µ–Є–Љ—Г—Й–µ—Б—В–≤`
  - Replace with the actual advantages.
- `–Ф–Њ–≤–µ—А–Є—В–µ–ї—М–љ—Л–µ –Њ—В–љ–Њ—И–µ–љ–Є—П`
  - Explain what builds that trust.
- `–°–≤—П–Ј—М —Б –∞—Г–і–Є—В–Њ—А–Є–µ–є`
  - Explain the behavior, not the abstraction.

## Editing rule

Do not perform cosmetic synonym swaps. If the phrase is empty, rewrite the whole sentence around a concrete thought.
