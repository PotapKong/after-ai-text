# Banned Phrases — English / English-first spillover

These words and openers often leak into translated or English-first drafts and make them sound synthetic.

## Overused words

- `delve`, `dive`, `deep dive`
- `realm`, `landscape`, `arena`, `the world of`
- `crucial`, `critical`, `essential`, `paramount`
- `seamless`, `robust`, `cutting-edge`, `game-changing`
- `tailored`, `bespoke`
- `testament`, `unwavering dedication`
- `foster`, `empower`, `enhance`, `elevate`
- `unlock`, `unleash`, `harness`, `leverage`
- `navigate`, `embark`
- `profound`, `deep` + abstract noun

## Overused openers and transitions

- `In today's fast-paced world`
- `In today's digital age`
- `It's important to note that`
- `Let's dive into`
- `Let's explore`
- `Furthermore`
- `Moreover`
- `Consequently`
- `Subsequently`
- `Nonetheless`
- `cannot be overstated`
- `here's the thing`
- `here's the kicker`

## Editing rule

Do not just replace one AI word with another prestigious synonym. Rewrite toward plain, specific language.
