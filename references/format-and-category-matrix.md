# Format and Category Matrix

Use this file to choose the right length and structure before rewriting.

## Length modes

### Short
Use for:
- short Telegram posts
- thoughts
- one-observation posts
- announcements with one angle

Typical shape:
- 3-6 short paragraphs
- one central idea
- minimal branching
- one clean ending, no over-explanation

Failure mode:
- trying to sound profound too quickly

### Medium
Use for:
- standard Telegram posts
- expert observations
- personal reflections
- speaker intros

Typical shape:
- 5-10 paragraphs
- one main thought plus development
- one or two supporting turns only
- ending should land simply

Failure mode:
- over-packaging the thought and polishing every paragraph

### Long
Use for:
- longreads
- articles
- extended explainers
- structured educational pieces

Typical shape:
- multiple blocks
- explicit logic progression
- examples or cases
- more factual scaffolding

Failure mode:
- dragging weak insight across too many paragraphs

## Category modes

### Observation post
Goal:
- capture one accurate observation

Best length:
- short or medium

Structure:
1. observation
2. brief unpacking
3. simple landing

Avoid:
- fake depth
- dramatic endings

### Personal reflection
Goal:
- show a real internal conclusion without over-performing it

Best length:
- medium

Structure:
1. personal context
2. what keeps repeating
3. what changed in how the author sees it
4. grounded ending

Avoid:
- aphorisms
- over-neat self-analysis

### Expert post
Goal:
- teach one important point clearly

Best length:
- medium or long

Structure:
1. practical claim
2. what people get wrong
3. correct frame
4. practical implication

Avoid:
- buzzwords
- vague “importance” language

### Speaker intro
Goal:
- present a speaker from the organizer point of view

Best length:
- short or medium

Structure:
1. why this slot matters for the event
2. who is in the program
3. why the audience should pay attention

Avoid:
- pretending to know the speaker’s style without evidence
- grand praise without substance
- literary introspection

### Promo / sales-adjacent post
Goal:
- create interest without sounding like ad sludge

Best length:
- short or medium

Structure:
1. relevant pain / desire / situation
2. concrete value
3. clear next step

Avoid:
- empty urgency
- fluffy promises

### Article / longread
Goal:
- explain a topic with enough depth to be genuinely useful

Best length:
- long

Structure:
1. frame the issue
2. break it into parts
3. support with examples or logic
4. close with a practical conclusion

Avoid:
- stretching a medium-level insight into an article

## Selection rule

Pick category first.
Pick length second.
Then rewrite to fit that container instead of defaulting to the same medium post shape every time.
