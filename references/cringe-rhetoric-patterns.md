# Cringe Rhetoric Patterns

This file collects rhetorical moves that often feel over-written, self-conscious, or AI-shaped.

## Avoid

### Quoted identity comparisons
- `я не как "X", я "Y"`
- `не "работаю ради денег", а "строю своё"`

These feel staged and smug.

### Multi-step contrast ladders
- `не общее впечатление, не "было интересно", а мысль...`
- any sentence that walks through two fake options before landing on the “real” one

These make the writing look performed.

### Slogan identity frames
- `я из тех, кто...`
- `я не про..., я про...`
- `мы не про..., мы про...`

Often too neat to feel true.

### Pre-built conclusion wrappers
- `вот такая мысль`
- `если коротко`
- `вывод простой`
- `поэтому главный вопрос`

Keep only when truly needed for clarity.

### Over-designed paragraph rhythm
- every paragraph ends like a quote card
- too many one-line “impact” paragraphs in a row

## Editing rule

If the line looks ready for a motivational carousel, flatten it.
